Erika & Kyle

After a mere eight years in friendship and love, we have decided to tie the knot. “Don’t be hasty,” our friends warned. “Give it another decade or two, see how it goes,” our families cautioned. “You’re not married?” asked the neighbors with surprised curiosity.

Against all odds and some advice, we’ll throw caution to the wind early this summer as we get married in Strasburg, Virginia. The nuptials will be private, but we welcome you and your guests to a celebratory cookout held shortly thereafter.

Let’s celebrate.

Join us at the American Legion River Lot in Strasburg, Virginia on June 7, 2014 at 3:00pm. You and your guests are welcome to attend all or part of the wonderful wedding weekend festivities. We’ll be the first to tell you this is not a formal wedding reception. There will be tents, camping, music, lawn games, food, and general tomfoolery of the most enjoyable kind. Should you decide to stay the night, feel free to pitch a tent for on-site camping. Celebratory cookouts aren’t for everyone.
